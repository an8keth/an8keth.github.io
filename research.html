Research

My research is focused on different methods of understanding singularities in commutative algebra. In particular, my interests include multiplicities, convex geometric methods, symbolic powers, homological methods and connections to combinatorics.

Papers

  1. Partial Betti splittings with applications to binomial edge ideals (with Adam Van Tuyl and A.V. Jayanthan)
    Collectanea Mathematica (2026). [arxiv]
    We introduce the notion of a partial betti splitting for graded ideals. We use this to obtain interesting results about the Betti numbers of binomial edge ideals.
